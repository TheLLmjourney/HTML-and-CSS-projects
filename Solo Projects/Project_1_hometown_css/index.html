Mumbai

Welcome to Mumbai, the City of Dreams, where a vibrant tapestry of history, culture, and relentless energy awaits you at every corner.

What to do in Mumbai

Elephanta Caves

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, these ancient rock-cut caves are located on an island a short ferry ride from the Gateway of India. They feature a stunning collection of sculpted temples dedicated primarily to the Hindu god Shiva.

Marine Drive

The C-shaped coastal road is famous for its panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. The streetlights create a dazzling string of lights at night, making it a favorite for evening strolls and sunset watching.

Indulge in a street food tour

Mumbai is a foodie's delight. Savor iconic street food like vada pav, pani puri, bhel puri, and pav bhaji at famous spots like Girgaon Chowpatty or Juhu Beach.

Witness the magic of Bollywood

As the capital of the Indian film industry, Mumbai offers various studio tours where you can go behind the scenes, see live sets, and learn about the filmmaking process.
